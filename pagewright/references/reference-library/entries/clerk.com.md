**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Headline-by-negation: open with "More than X" to reframe yourself into a larger category than the one buyers pigeonhole you into — pairs a familiar anchor word (authentication) with an upgrade (User Management).
- Demo the actual product as the imagery: instead of stylized illustrations, drop real, pixel-perfect UI components (sign-in card, profile modal, 'Secured by you' badge) so the page itself proves the polish you're selling.

- Ration the accent: keep the entire page near-monochrome and spend your one saturated gradient purple exclusively on the primary CTA so it reads as the single obvious next action.
- Pair the primary CTA with a secondary 'Build with agents' split-button — acknowledge the AI-coding-agent workflow as a first-class entry path alongside the human one.

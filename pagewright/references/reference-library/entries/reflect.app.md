# Reflect — https://reflect.app

![reflect.app](../thumbs/reflect.app.jpg)

- **niche:** productivity
- **mood:** technical-dark
- **style:** dark, gradient, glass
- **palette:** bg `#0B0A1A` · ink `#EDEAF7` · accent `#7C5CFF` — Primary CTA buttons (Start free trial), the radiant violet black-hole glow in the hero, active sidebar/calendar selection states, inline link underlines, and the New AI announcement pill
- **type:** display *Aeonik Pro* · body *Inter (Inter V variable)* — Geometric grotesque display with humanist warmth; large optical-size headline set at light/regular weight reads calm and premium, not shouty. Body Inter keeps UI legible and neutral.
- **sections:** hero › feature-ai-assistant › feature-superpowers › feature-never-lose-info › feature › feature-security › feature-meetings › logos › testimonials › team › feature-academy › pricing › cta › footer
- **signature:** A glowing violet black-hole / event-horizon gradient sits dead center under the headline, and the product UI screenshot rises directly out of its light — the cosmic glow physically bleeds up into the app window so the tool looks like it's emerging from a singularity of thought.
- **imagery:** Product-screenshot-led, fused with an abstract gradient-mesh light source. The real Reflect note-taking UI (sidebar, daily-note outline, calendar) is shown in a dark theme and color-matched so the hero's violet bloom appears to illuminate it from below. A play-button overlay invites a demo video. Particle/starfield dots and a faint orbital arc reinforce the space metaphor.
- **copy:** Aspirational outcome-first voice — sells the cognitive benefit, not the feature list. Hero headline: "Think better with Reflect" with subhead "Never miss a note, idea or connection."

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Light/regular-weight giant display type on near-black reads more premium than heavy bold — let size, not weight, carry the headline.
- Make the hero glow and the product screenshot share one color so the UI looks lit by the brand light; the screenshot emerging from the gradient is the whole trick.
- Wrap nav in a single floating rounded pill and echo that pill shape in the announcement badge and CTA for a tight, consistent rounded-rectangle system.
- Lead every section with a benefit-as-promise headline ('Give your brain superpowers', 'Never lose information') instead of naming the feature — let the screenshot name the feature.

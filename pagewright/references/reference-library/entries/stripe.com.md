- **style:** gradient, minimal, colorful
- **palette:** bg `#FFFFFF` · ink `#0A2540` · accent `#635BFF` — Primary 'Get started' CTA button fill and the 'Contact sales' button in nav; secondary line of the hero headline is set in a muted slate-blue (#425466) rather than the purple — the real visual accent is the corner gradient ribbon (orange-to-magenta-to-periwinkle) bleeding in from the top-right

- **signature:** The single oversized gradient ribbon that enters from the top-right corner only — a diagonal feathered sweep of orange→magenta→periwinkle laid over a pure-white canvas, so 80% of the hero stays empty and editorial while one corner does all the emotional work. Bonus move: a live counter in the eyebrow ('Global GDP running on Stripe: 1.65837609%') ticking real decimals.
- **imagery:** Gradient-mesh as the hero hero. A single high-fidelity, lightly blurred fluid/silk gradient sweep used as a corner motif rather than a full-bleed background. No product screenshots, photography, or illustration in the hero. Customer logos rendered in their real brand colors on white (Amazon, NVIDIA, Ford, Coinbase, Google, Shopify, Mindbody). Downstream sections lean on product-screenshot and code/API UI per the feature headings.
- **copy:** Declarative infrastructure-as-promise, then immediately concrete: hero reads 'Financial infrastructure to grow your revenue. Accept payments, offer financial services, and implement custom revenue models—from your first transaction to your billionth.' — abstract noun phrase + plain-spoken capability list, voice is calm, technical, and quietly grandiose.

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Confine the color to one corner: a pure-white stage with a single feathered gradient ribbon bleeding in from top-right reads richer than a full gradient background, and keeps headline contrast bulletproof.
- Two-tone the headline: set the promise line in near-black ink and the supporting clause in a muted slate so a long multi-line H1 still has hierarchy without changing size.
- Pair the CTA with a low-friction 'Sign up with Google' button beside the brand-purple primary — gives an enterprise audience an instant-start path next to 'Get started'.
- Put a credibility flex in the tiny eyebrow above the H1 (a live decimal counter like 'Global GDP running on Stripe: 1.658%') — quantified, oddly precise, and animated to imply real-time scale.
- Run customer logos in full brand color on white instead of greyed-out — at Stripe's logo tier the color reads as 'these brands chose us', not visual noise.

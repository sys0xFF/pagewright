**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Split the fold 50/50: flat brand-color panel for type on one side, a full-bleed illustrated world holding the product on the other — no hero photo required.
- Arc-set the wordmark on a curve to evoke a bottle cap / badge; it instantly signals heritage and craft for a consumable brand.
- Lean on a single warm accent (buttery yellow) for every pill CTA and let it recur inside the illustration's sunset so the button feels native to the scene.
- Sell function with nostalgia: triple-anaphora copy ("Real ingredients. Real refreshment. Real digestive health support.") makes a gut-health claim feel like an old-fashioned soda promise.

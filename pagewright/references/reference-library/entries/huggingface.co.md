# Huggingface — https://huggingface.co

![huggingface.co](../thumbs/huggingface.co.jpg)

- **niche:** ai
- **mood:** technical-dark
- **style:** dark, mono-type, minimal
- **palette:** bg `#FFFFFF` · ink `#1B1B1D` · accent `#FFD21E` — the hugging-face emoji brand mark (logo, hero glyph, 'Trending on 🤗 this week' inline), plus warm yellow/orange flame and heart micro-icons scattered through the model listing panel
- **type:** display *Source Sans Pro (heavy/black weight, very tight tracking)* · body *Source Sans Pro regular; IBM Plex Mono for code/model-name UI labels* — Friendly-engineering: a chunky humanist sans for warmth in the headline, paired with a monospace that signals 'this is a tool for builders' in every chip and repo name.
- **sections:** hero › feature-models › feature-spaces › feature-datasets › feature-platform › how-it-works › logos › feature-open-source › feature-enterprise › feature-inference › feature-compute › cta › footer
- **signature:** The hero isn't a polished marketing mockup — it's a tilted, live-feeling slice of the actual product UI (the real model-browser with filter chips, parameter slider, and a scrolling repo feed) bleeding off the right edge. It sells the platform by showing the dense, working tool itself instead of an abstracted illustration.
- **imagery:** No stock photography or 3D renders. Imagery = the product's own dense interface rendered as a dark rounded-corner panel, layered with tiny colorful framework/provider logos (PyTorch, JAX, Groq, Cerebras) as monochrome-leaning chips. The single emoji glyph carries all the brand personality against an otherwise clinical, data-grid aesthetic.
- **copy:** Plain, mission-grade declarative — quietly ambitious, no hype words. Hero: "The AI community building the future." sub: "The platform where the machine learning community collaborates on models, datasets, and applications."

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Use a real, slightly-tilted slice of your live product as hero art and let it bleed off the canvas edge — density reads as credibility for a builder audience.
- Let ONE saturated brand glyph (here the yellow emoji) do all the warmth work on an otherwise austere monochrome page, even reusing it inline inside section headings ('Trending on 🤗 this week').
- Pair a heavy humanist display sans with a monospace UI font so the page feels both human and engineered — mono in chips/repo-names signals 'tool', sans in the headline signals 'community'.
- Twin CTAs as 'primary button OR plain text link' ('Explore AI Apps  or  Browse 2M+ models') — the secondary path doubles as a live proof-of-scale stat.

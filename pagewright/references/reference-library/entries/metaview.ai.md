# Metaview — https://metaview.ai

![metaview.ai](../thumbs/metaview.ai.jpg)

- **niche:** ai recruiting / hr-tech (agentic SaaS)
- **mood:** technical-dark
- **style:** dark, gradient, cinematic
- **palette:** bg `#0A1410` · ink `#F4F7F4` · accent `#5AF0A0` — primary CTA pill (Start for free), atmospheric green horizon glow behind the hero, small inline status/tag chips in the product UI
- **type:** display *Georgia* · body *Inter* — Editorial serif headline at giant scale set against clean neutral-grotesque body — a literary, confident, almost newspaper-masthead tone that feels human against a machine-built product
- **sections:** hero › logos › feature-overview › feature-agents › how-it-works › feature-security › testimonials › feature-roadmap › pricing › faq › cta › footer
- **signature:** A black-hole / planetary horizon: the hero sits over a dark sphere with a luminous green atmospheric rim-light curving across the lower third, as if you're looking down on a planet's edge at dawn — celestial scale standing in for "AI that sees everything," instead of the flat purple-gradient hero every AI SaaS defaults to.
- **imagery:** Cinematic dark-space gradient backdrop with a single curved green light source; foreground is a realistic embedded product UI panel (dark glass card) showing the actual sourcing flow — candidate cards with real photos, source/research tags, an agent message. Product-as-hero, photographed-feeling lighting rather than abstract illustration.
- **copy:** Bold serif manifesto headline, plain-spoken and sports/team-coded ("AI agents for winning teams."); subhead is concrete and benefit-dense ("the Agentic Recruiting Platform built for radically efficient hiring, end to end"), with a recurring human-vs-toil voice ("Agents that love the tasks you hate").

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Set a huge editorial SERIF headline (Georgia) over a dark tech backdrop — the serif/dark contrast reads premium and human, breaking the sans-on-gradient AI cliche.
- Use one curved atmospheric light (planet-rim glow) as the entire hero composition instead of busy gradients — a single light source does more work than ten.
- Embed the literal working product UI (candidate cards, real photos, agent chat) right under the fold so the abstract 'AI agent' claim is instantly made concrete.
- Reserve a single high-chroma neon green for ONE primary CTA and let the rest stay near-monochrome dark — accent scarcity makes the button magnetic.

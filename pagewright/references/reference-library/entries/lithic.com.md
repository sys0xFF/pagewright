- **style:** dark, 3d, cinematic, mono-type
- **palette:** bg `#0A0A0A` · ink `#FFFFFF` · accent `#F26A1B` — primary CTA pill ('Contact us') and embossed circuit-line glow within the floating cards; used sparingly as the single warm spark against an all-black field

- **sections:** hero › logos › feature-reliability › feature-customization › feature-compliance › feature-settlements › how-it-works › testimonials › developer-cta › cta › footer
- **signature:** The hero is a single cinematic 3D scene: a spiral cascade of matte-black credit cards orbiting along concentric circuit-etched arcs, lit only by faint orange filament lines — turning an abstract API product into one physical, gravity-defying object instead of the usual fintech dashboard-screenshot collage.
- **imagery:** Photoreal 3D render of identical black-on-black physical cards spiraling in deep space, with hairline concentric guide-lines and subtle orange edge-lighting tracing circuitry. Monochrome, near-zero color, high contrast — product-as-sculpture rather than UI mockups or stock photography.
- **copy:** Plainspoken infrastructure confidence with a wry edge ("Payments are complex. Your programs don't need to be."); hero: "Programmable card issuing and money movement"

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Render your abstract/API product as ONE physical hero object in deep-black space — a spiral of repeated hardware lit by a single accent color reads as premium and tactile without a single UI screenshot
- Run a near-monochrome palette (black + white) and reserve ONE warm accent (orange) for exactly two things: the primary CTA pill and the product's glow — scarcity makes the spark land
- Pair a tight grotesque display face with a humanist body to signal 'engineered, not cute' — escapes the rounded-friendly default of fintech branding
- Use concentric circuit-line motifs as both background texture and the lighting source for the hero object, so the decoration and the product are visually one system

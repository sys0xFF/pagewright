- **niche:** fintech
- **mood:** editorial-minimal
- **style:** editorial-minimal, mono-type, warm-neutral
- **palette:** bg `#F7F5F0` · ink `#1A1A1A` · accent `#F5C518` — Tiny arrow glyphs inside CTA buttons, the 'NEW' tag in the top announcement bar, and small icon accents — never large fills
- **type:** display *serrifCondensed (high-contrast condensed serif, used at huge scale for the H1 and section heads)* · body *Inter (and Tiempos Text for some editorial body copy)* — Old-world publishing authority meets modern fintech precision — a literary, magazine-cover serif headline carrying the trust signal, balanced by clean neutral sans for the functional supporting text
- **sections:** announcement-bar › hero › logos › feature-merchant-of-record › feature-billing › feature-profitwell-metrics › feature-retain › problem › testimonials › how-it-works › case-study › pricing › cta › footer
- **signature:** A massive high-contrast condensed serif headline ("Sell globally. Grow without the complexity.") rendered like a literary book cover on a warm cream paper background — rejecting the cold blue-gradient-and-sans convention that defines nearly every payments/fintech site.
- **imagery:** Sparse and restrained: monochrome customer wordmark logos (HUBX, MacPaw, Runn) set in a quiet row, product UI peeking from below the fold on a soft off-white card. No stock photos, no abstract 3D — the typography itself is the hero image. Warm paper-toned canvas instead of clinical white.
- **copy:** Confident, plain-spoken benefit statements in two short declaratives; hero reads "Sell globally. Grow without the complexity." with subhead positioning Paddle as "the Merchant of Record for digital product businesses."

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Use a single oversized literary serif headline as the entire hero visual on a warm cream (not white) ground — let type carry the brand instead of imagery or gradients
- Confine your accent to micro-moments only: a yellow arrow glyph inside the button and a 'NEW' tag, so the one bright color reads as deliberate, not decorative
- Pair a high-contrast condensed display serif with neutral Inter body for an 'editorial fintech' tension — authority up top, clarity below
- Stack two punchy two-word/short declarative sentences as the H1 instead of one long value-prop line — it scans like a magazine cover and slows the reader down

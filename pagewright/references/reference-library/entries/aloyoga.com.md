Section header below: "TRENDING NOW". A small "Atenção" notice flags the official Brazilian store (aloyoga.com.br), confirming this is the localized BR site.

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Build the hero as a multi-frame editorial triptych (cropped + framed + cropped) instead of one full-bleed image — it instantly reads as a styled fashion drop, not a generic banner.
- Pull your single accent color out of the campaign/product name itself ("Sun Kissed Brown") so the palette and the copy reinforce one seasonal idea.
- Keep the accent OFF the CTA: a plain white-and-black pill under the imagery feels more boutique than a colored button would.
- Pair a high-contrast italic serif headline with a tiny all-caps sans kicker ("NEW DOUBLE DROP") to get luxury-magazine hierarchy in two lines.

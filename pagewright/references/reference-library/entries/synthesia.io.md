- **imagery:** Real AI-avatar video frames (a presenter's face) embedded inside a soft, layered blue device mockup with rounded corners and tinted borders; floating UI chips and a translucent gradient sweep behind everything keep it light and airy, not a flat dashboard screenshot.

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Two-tone headline: keep the noun phrase in ink and color only the product category ('AI Video platform') to brand the words themselves
- Replace the static hero shot with a tabbed live demo (Create/Edit/Translate...) so visitors mentally walk the workflow before scrolling
- Stack social proof as a sandwich: G2 badge above the headline, 'No credit card required + 4.7/5' micro-proof under the CTAs
- Frame trust for three buyer personas in one line ('Loved by users. Approved by legal. Signed off by CFOs') then back it with a compliance row (SOC2/GDPR/ISO42001)

The product literally IS the visual.
- **imagery:** Photoreal product UI: a 3/4 isometric-tilted laptop-less dashboard floating off the page edge, oversized numeric KPI tiles (45.23s in red, 0.103% in amber), honeycomb host maps, and colorful time-series. Maximalist data density rendered as the hero art.
- **copy:** Plain-spoken capability claim with a confident promise — H1 'AI-Powered Observability and Security' over subhead 'See inside any stack, any app, at any scale, anywhere.'

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Use your real, busy dashboard as the hero — tilt it in perspective and let it bleed past the canvas edge so density reads as capability, not clutter
- Pair a buzzword H1 ('AI-Powered Observability') with a rhythmic, anaphoric subhead ('any stack, any app, any scale, anywhere') to make the promise stick
- Lean on oversized live KPI tiles in saturated red/amber as focal anchors inside an otherwise white, restrained layout
- Two-tier CTA: a solid purple 'Free Trial' next to a ghost 'See the Platform' with a play triangle — commit vs. explore in one row

- **niche:** productivity
- **mood:** warm-playful
- **style:** minimal, illustrated, colorful
- **palette:** bg `#FFFFFF` · ink `#0A0A0A` · accent `#3B7BF6` — Primary CTA buttons (Create form, Create a free form) and inline text links inside the demo form; a secondary hot-magenta (#FF1FB6) shows up as a hand-drawn squiggle underline and doodle accents
- **type:** display *Inter (heavy/black weight, tight tracking)* · body *Inter (regular)* — Geometric grotesque set ultra-bold and oversized for the headline, then quiet and neutral for body — the weight contrast does all the shouting while the typeface itself stays sober and Swiss
- **sections:** hero › feature-form-builder › feature-simple-powerful › feature-intelligent-logic › feature-customize › feature-share › feature-integrations › feature-personas › cta › faq › footer
- **signature:** The hero floats freehand marker doodles — a scribbled speech bubble, a hand-drawn 'Yes!', stars, arrows, a squiggle underline in hot magenta — scattered loosely in the white margins around an otherwise austere black-on-white headline. A form builder (a category that screams sterile enterprise SaaS) instead presents itself like a notebook someone doodled in.
- **imagery:** No photography and no polished 3D. Two visual registers collide: (1) loose, off-black ink doodles (speech bubbles, stars, arrows, squiggles) hand-scattered in the whitespace, and (2) a pixel-accurate live product mockup framed in a faux browser chrome (traffic-light dots) showing a real 'Register now' form mid-edit with drag handles and toolbar icons visible — selling the product literally rather than abstractly.
- **copy:** Plain-spoken, confident, anti-corporate — sells simplicity by being simple. Hero: 'The simplest way to create forms' with sub 'Say goodbye to boring forms. Meet Tally — the free, intuitive form builder you've been looking for.'

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Disarm a sterile category with hand-drawn marginalia: keep the type system rigorously clean and Swiss, then let imperfect ink doodles in the negative space carry all the personality and warmth.
- Use a single hot-magenta squiggle underline as the lone burst of saturated color under an all-black headline — one accent, placed once, reads bolder than a full color palette.
- Put the actual product UI in the hero, mid-edit with drag handles and toolbar chrome visible, instead of a vague abstract graphic — show the verb the product does.
- Stack a friction-killer micro-line ('No signup required') directly under the CTA to convert the simplicity promise into an immediate, low-commitment action.

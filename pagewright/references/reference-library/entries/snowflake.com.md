# Snowflake — https://snowflake.com

![snowflake.com](../thumbs/snowflake.com.jpg)

- **niche:** data-ai (enterprise data cloud / data warehousing)
- **mood:** bold-loud
- **style:** dark, photographic, cinematic, mono-type
- **palette:** bg `#1A9BD7` · ink `#FFFFFF` · accent `#11567F` — Faceted low-poly mountain peaks rendered in stepped blues (navy to sky) form the entire hero backdrop; the brand snowflake mark and primary CTA outline pick up the same cyan
- **type:** display *Texta (geometric grotesque, heavy weight, all-caps)* · body *Lato (humanist sans)* — Confident and industrial — a blunt all-caps condensed-feeling headline paired with a soft, friendly humanist body. The contrast reads as 'serious infrastructure, approachable to humans.'
- **sections:** topbar-announcement › nav › hero › feature-event-stage › feature-simplify › feature-architecture › logos › feature-ecosystem › blog-latest › cta-where-data-does-more › footer
- **signature:** The hero is built entirely from a low-poly, geometric mountain-range illustration in stacked translucent blue facets — the brand's snowflake logo literally becomes the snow-capped summit. A data-warehouse company renders its homepage as a literal landscape (a real photo of the CEO bleeds through the faceted glass on the right), refusing the abstract-gradient-mesh cliché every other data/AI company uses.
- **imagery:** Two layers fused: vector low-poly geometry (triangular mountain facets, floating cloud glyphs) overlaid on a real human photograph (a suited executive) that shows through the translucent shapes. Editorial brand-event treatment — feels like a conference poster, not a product shot. Secondary band uses a duotone-blue keynote stage photo.
- **copy:** Aspirational, plain-spoken declarative in all-caps — leads with the mission, not the feature. Hero headline: "MAKING AI REAL FOR BUSINESS"

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Turn your logo into the scenery: let the brand mark become a literal element of an illustrated landscape (snowflake = mountain peak) instead of sitting in the corner.
- Layer translucent vector facets over a real photo so geometry and humanity coexist — avoids both the cold abstract-mesh look and the generic stock-photo look.
- Restrain to a single-hue universe (every shade is a blue) and let the all-caps display weight, not color, carry the loudness.
- Lead the hero with a mission statement ('Making AI Real for Business') and demote the actual product to lower bands — sell the vision before the warehouse.

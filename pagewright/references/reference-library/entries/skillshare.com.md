- **niche:** education
- **mood:** warm-playful
- **style:** colorful, photographic, friendly, signup-forward
- **palette:** bg `#FFFFFF` · ink `#0E0E0E` · accent `#00FF84` — A bright mint-green reserved tightly for the top-right "Inscreva-se" pill and the thin underline beneath "grátis" in the eyebrow; everything else (the rainbow ribbon, the tile photos) is decoration, so the green stays the single "go" signal.
- **type:** display *geometric grotesque, Bouygues/Founders-style — plausibly Circular or a custom "Skillshare Sans"* · body *humanist sans, GT Walsheim / Inter neighbor* — Warm and rounded, conversational rather than corporate; the wordmark sets "SKILL / share." in two heavy stacked lines with a green dot.
- **sections:** hero › category-tiles › popular-classes-carousel › value-props › teacher-spotlight › testimonials › membership-pricing › cta › footer
- **signature:** The fold is split: a clean white left column with the headline sits beside a fully-built OAuth signup card (Google, Facebook, Apple, e-mail) — the conversion form IS the hero, not a downstream step. Underneath, a full-width filmstrip of saturated category tiles ("Ilustração", "Animação", "Filmes e vídeos", "Freelancer") mixing flat illustration with real maker photography runs edge-to-edge, and a hand-drawn multicolor gradient ribbon swoops behind the type from the left margin — that loose, painterly squiggle is what breaks it out of standard SaaS sterility.
- **imagery:** Mixed media on purpose: a freeform gradient brush-ribbon (teal→blue→magenta→yellow) as background flourish, bold flat character illustration in some tiles, and warm candid photos of real creators (a man in a cap, someone in sunglasses, hands on a drawing tablet) in others — high color, no 3D, no UI screenshots.
- **copy:** Plain, encouraging, creator-to-creator — headline "Aulas criativas ensinadas pelos melhores profissionais criativos", eyebrow "Ganhe 7 dias grátis de Skillshare" (the free-trial hook stated before any pitch). Localized Portuguese; the OAuth card carries fine print about Termos de Serviço and Política de Privacidade.

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Put the actual signup card (social OAuth + email) directly in the fold beside the headline so the hero doubles as the conversion form.
- Lead the eyebrow with the offer ("7 days free") and underline just the one word that matters in the accent color — a single deliberate green mark.
- Run an edge-to-edge filmstrip of saturated category tiles immediately under the fold to telegraph breadth instantly, before any copy explains it.
- Hand-draw one loose multicolor gradient ribbon behind clean type to inject warmth and craft into an otherwise white, grid-clean layout.

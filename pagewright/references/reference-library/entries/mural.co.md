- **niche:** productivity
- **mood:** technical-dark
- **style:** dark, editorial, colorful
- **palette:** bg `#0A0A0A` · ink `#FFFFFF` · accent `#3DDC97` — primary CTA button fill ('Get started free' / 'Sign up free' chevron), plus a rainbow multi-color accent set (red/yellow/green/blue) reserved for the Mural logo and the in-canvas diagram strokes
- **type:** display *Serif (high-contrast transitional serif, GT Sectra / Tiempos-like)* · body *Neo-grotesque sans-serif (Inter / Helvetica Now-like)* — Editorial confidence meets utilitarian clarity — a literary serif headline carries the emotion while a neutral sans keeps the product copy crisp and trustworthy
- **sections:** hero › logos › feature-vision › results-proof › feature-ai-workflows › feature-team-outcomes › solutions › transformation › testimonials › blog-insights › release-notes › pricing-sizes › cta › footer
- **signature:** A two-tone serif headline where the first half ('Sync up. Speed up.') sits in muted grey and the punchline ('Stand out.') snaps to bright white on the line below — using value contrast, not size, to deliver the rhetorical payoff. A literary serif on a near-black canvas is a deliberate break from the flat-sans, light-mode convention of the whiteboard/collaboration category.
- **imagery:** A live, floating product-canvas screenshot bleeds up from the bottom edge — hand-drawn-style mind maps with red/blue/green connector strokes, real LinkedIn-style persona cards (headshots, names, titles), and color-coded department clusters (Marketing, Finance). Overlaid on top-right is a rounded white interactive 'Which team are you part of?' card with selectable tiles, blending real UI with a personalization quiz to make the hero feel like the app itself.
- **copy:** Punchy three-beat imperative tagline with an editorial serif voice; hero reads 'Sync up. Speed up. Stand out.' followed by an empathetic 'You're busier than ever' subhead positioning Mural as the 'visual AI platform.'

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Split a headline into muted setup + bright-white punchline on separate lines — let value contrast (grey to white), not font size, carry the climax.
- Pair a high-contrast literary serif display with a neutral grotesque body on a near-black canvas to feel editorial and premium while staying readable for product copy.
- Embed an interactive personalization widget ('Which team are you part of?') directly into the hero so the first scroll already feels like using the product, and route visitors to tailored value.
- Keep the UI chrome monochrome (white CTA-green only) but reserve a full rainbow accent strictly for the logo and live in-canvas strokes, signaling 'this is where color/creativity lives.'

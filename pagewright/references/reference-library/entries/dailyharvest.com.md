# Daily Harvest — https://www.daily-harvest.com

![dailyharvest.com](../thumbs/dailyharvest.com.jpg)

- **niche:** food
- **mood:** clean-light
- **style:** editorial, photographic, minimal, warm-neutral
- **palette:** bg `#F2EFE9` · ink `#1C1A17` · accent `#000000` — There is no marketing color at all: the only "accent" is pure black, used on the solid `SHOP STARTER BOX` button, the `LOG IN` pill, and the thin top promo bar. Color comes entirely from the food photo (the pink smoothie), not from the UI.
- **type:** display *high-contrast editorial serif with a swash italic (Canela / Ogg / Tiempos Headline feel)* · body *small humanist sans (Founders Grotesk / Neue Haas)* — Magazine-cover poise; the serif does the talking, the sans just labels.
- **sections:** hero › shop-collections › how-it-works › ingredients-quality › reviews/social-proof › cta › footer
- **signature:** The headline mixes upright and italic inside one phrase — "Eat *better,* every day." — with "better" set in a swooping serif italic so a single word carries all the warmth. It sits flush-right next to a near-full-bleed photo of a blender mid-pour (pink smoothie, soft kitchen bokeh), and the whole composition is split hard down the middle: editorial photo left, centered text column right on warm cream. A 5-star row plus the eyebrow "LOVED BY OVER 2 MILLION CUSTOMERS" does the trust work above the title.
- **imagery:** Photographic, naturalistic, lifestyle. A real blender of strawberry-peach smoothie shot in a warm-lit home kitchen with shallow depth-of-field — no 3D, no illustration, no product render. There's a small circular pause/play control overlaid on the image, implying the hero is a looping video, not a still.
- **copy:** Warm, imperative, plain-spoken. Headline "Eat *better,* every day."; eyebrow "LOVED BY OVER 2 MILLION CUSTOMERS"; subhead "Organic smoothies & bowls, built on fruits and vegetables, ready in minutes. No subscription required." A scrolling marquee of credentials ("GLUTEN-FREE · CERTIFIED ORGANIC · DAIRY-FREE") runs under the fold, and the section below opens "SHOP COLLECTIONS / A high protein breakfast, easy meal swap, light snack, or evening ritual. Ready when you are."

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Italicize ONE word inside an otherwise-upright serif headline to inject voice without a second typeface or any color.
- Go fully colorless in the UI (black + cream only) and let a single appetizing food photo be the only source of saturation.
- Make the hero a silent looping video of the product in motion (a pour, a stir) with a tiny pause control, not a frozen still.
- Stack trust above the headline: star row + "loved by 2M customers" eyebrow before the brand even makes its pitch.
- Run an infinite-scroll marquee of certifications ("certified organic", "dairy-free") as a low-key, repeating credibility band between hero and collections.

# Tedy — https://tedy.app

![tedy.app](../thumbs/tedy.app.jpg)

- **niche:** hr-tech (employee benefits / compensation & rewards platform)
- **mood:** warm-playful
- **style:** colorful, photographic, bento
- **palette:** bg `#FFFFFF` · ink `#111111` · accent `#EC2D7A` — primary CTA buttons (Book a demo, Sign in pill, cookie accept), brand mark dot, small play-button glyph
- **type:** display *Geometric grotesque sans (DM Sans / Hanken Grotesk-like), heavy weight* · body *Same family, regular weight* — Friendly, rounded-terminal grotesque set extra-bold and tight — confident and approachable, not corporate-stiff
- **sections:** hero › logos › feature-intro
- **signature:** A pastel-lilac rounded card as the hero's left half, paired with a real-photo bento grid of laughing, candid people on the right — the page leads with human joy instead of the dashboard-screenshot-on-dark that dominates HR/comp SaaS.
- **imagery:** Authentic, warm lifestyle photography of diverse real people mid-laugh, arranged in a varied-size rounded bento grid (one large vertical hero photo plus two stacked smaller frames). No product UI screenshots in view; logos in the trust strip are muted greyscale.
- **copy:** Benefit-led, plain-spoken value claim — hero headline "More value from every compensation dollar." with a punchy staccato subhead "Let employees choose. Same budget. Higher impact. Ready in minutes."

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Pair an oversized extra-bold headline against a soft pastel card, then balance it with real human photography in a bento grid — warmth instead of a dark product shot.
- Use staccato fragment subheads ('Same budget. Higher impact. Ready in minutes.') to compress the pitch into rhythmic, scannable beats.
- Anchor the brand in a single hot magenta-pink accent used sparingly on CTAs and the logo dot, so it pops against an otherwise white/lilac neutral field.
- Mix a pill-shaped solid primary CTA with a white outline secondary that carries a tiny accent play-glyph — clear hierarchy without a second loud color.

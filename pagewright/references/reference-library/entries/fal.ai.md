- **imagery:** No product UI or photography in the hero. Instead: flat-color abstract vector art — pixelated lo-fi blobs, diagonal yellow/purple ribbons, and scattered pixel "confetti" — sitting on a saturated cyan plane. Decorative, brand-driven, generative-art-coded rather than literal.

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Treat the H1 as a 4-line typographic poster: massive near-black grotesk, hyphen-breaking the words, leading tight enough that lines kiss — type becomes the hero image.
- Defy the category's default mood: pick a single saturated, unexpected background color (here electric cyan) and commit the whole hero to it, no gradient safety net.
- Use lo-fi 'pixel' / 8-bit decorative blobs as brand texture to humanize hard infra — it signals 'playful + generative' without showing a single screenshot.
- Pair one bright primary (yellow) against the cool field for high-energy accent ribbons, keeping ink pure black for maximum legibility over color.

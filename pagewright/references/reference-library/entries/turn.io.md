# Turn — https://turn.io

![turn.io](../thumbs/turn.io.jpg)

- **niche:** healthtech
- **mood:** premium-luxe
- **style:** dark, photographic, colorful
- **palette:** bg `#0E3A2A` · ink `#DCEFE4` · accent `#F2622E` — Primary CTA button only ('Book a Call'); a secondary teal-green (#37C16E) accents the highlighted headline word and metric numbers
- **type:** display *DM Sans* · body *DM Sans / Arial* — geometric humanist sans, very large soft-cornered weights; friendly, clean, approachable rather than clinical
- **sections:** hero › logos › problem › feature-automate › how-it-works › testimonials › cta-accelerator › footer
- **signature:** Floating live-chat UI chips (greeting bubble + PDF prescription pill) hovering over patient/clinician photos — staging the WhatsApp conversation as the product instead of showing a dashboard screenshot.
- **imagery:** Photography-led: warm documentary photos of African patients and clinicians in rounded-rectangle cards, layered with floating WhatsApp UI chips (chat bubble "Hi Amina!", a PDF prescription attachment) to dramatize the product without a literal screenshot.
- **copy:** Outcome-as-product headline with a teal "WhatsApp" highlight word. Hero: "Turn WhatsApp into your Health App" — confident, benefit-first, names the platform patients already use.

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Pair a deep forest-green canvas with a single hot-orange CTA so the one action you want is the only warm thing on screen — everything else recedes.
- Highlight one verb/noun in the headline in a brighter saturated tint (teal here) to encode the core promise in a single colored word.
- Replace the obligatory product screenshot with cropped chat-UI chips (greeting bubble, file attachment) floating over real human photos — sells the messaging product more warmly than a UI mockup.
- Lead the proof section with hard outcome metrics phrased as deltas (567%, 65%, -13%, '10 min to 9 sec') in the bright accent green, tied directly to quoted operator testimonials.

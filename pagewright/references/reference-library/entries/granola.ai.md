- **niche:** productivity
- **mood:** warm-playful
- **style:** minimal, mono-type, illustrated
- **palette:** bg `#FAF9F6` · ink `#2B2B28` · accent `#5C6B1E` — primary 'Download for Mac' CTA fill (olive granola-green), sparkle/dot icons, link and badge highlights
- **type:** display *High-contrast transitional serif (Tiempos-like)* · body *Geometric grotesque sans (Aktiv Grotesk-like)* — Editorial-bookish serif headline over clean utilitarian sans — reads like a smart notebook, not a dashboard. Warm, literate, calm.
- **sections:** hero › logos › how-it-works › feature-transcribe › feature-enhance › feature-platforms › feature-templates › feature-integrations › feature-share › testimonials › cta › footer
- **signature:** The hero is a literal before/after diptych of the product's own output — "Your notes + transcript" (raw) beside "AI enhanced" (polished) — so the product demos itself instead of a stylized hero graphic doing the talking.
- **imagery:** Skeuomorphic note-window cards with macOS traffic-light dots and serif headings inside — UI mimics paper/Apple Notes. Real headshots in testimonials, tiny sparkle/dot motifs. Imagery IS the product surface, not abstract 3D.
- **copy:** Plain, human, slightly witty ("Not the granola kind."); hero leads with the user's pain not the tech: "The AI notepad for people in back-to-back meetings"

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Lead with a literal before/after diptych of your own product output rather than a hero illustration — let the artifact be the pitch.
- Pair a high-contrast editorial serif headline with a neutral sans body on a warm off-white (#FAF9F6) paper canvas to read 'thoughtful tool' not 'tech startup'.
- Borrow a desktop-app shell (macOS traffic-light dots, window frames) as the imagery system so screenshots feel native and trustworthy.
- Pick an unexpected, food-derived accent (olive 'granola' green) that ties to the brand name and stays warm — avoid the default SaaS blue/purple.

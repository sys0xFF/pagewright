# Whimsical — https://whimsical.com

![whimsical.com](../thumbs/whimsical.com.jpg)

- **niche:** productivity
- **mood:** warm-playful
- **style:** gradient, cinematic, minimal
- **palette:** bg `#C9A9D4` · ink `#2A1A3E` · accent `#7C3AED` — logo gem, primary CTA buttons (dark pill with purple arrow), Share button in product UI, and the violet-to-coral gradient that fills the entire hero stage
- **type:** display *Agrandir* · body *Manrope* — The display is a chunky, soft-cornered grotesque with friendly rounded terminals — playful and confident without being childish; Manrope keeps body copy crisp and neutral so the warmth lives entirely in the headline
- **sections:** nav › announcement-bar › hero › feature-diagrams › feature-wireframes › feature-infinite-canvas › feature-flowcharts › feature-mind-maps › feature-whimsical-ai › feature-grid › cta › footer
- **signature:** The hero is a literal sunset — a violet-to-coral macOS-wallpaper gradient that the actual product screenshot rises into from the bottom edge, as if the canvas is an horizon. It trades the flat white "tool screenshot on grid" cliché of every diagramming/whiteboard SaaS for an atmospheric, almost emotional landscape.
- **imagery:** One enormous, photorealistic hi-fi product screenshot anchored at the bottom of the hero, angled subtly and bleeding off the lower edge so it reads as a horizon line under the gradient sky. The shot is dense and real (flowcharts, sticky notes, mind maps, live multiplayer avatars, a Share button) — it sells the product by showing genuine working complexity rather than a sanitized empty state.
- **copy:** Aspirational and human, not feature-led — leads with the feeling of creation then qualifies the audience: "Where great ideas take shape" / sub: "Whimsical is a purpose-built whiteboard for technical teams and agents."

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Replace the flat-white tool screenshot with an atmospheric gradient 'sky' and let the product UI rise into it like a horizon — turns a utilitarian app shot into a mood piece.
- Pair a soft, rounded chunky display face (Agrandir) with a neutral geometric body (Manrope): all the brand warmth concentrates in the headline while UI copy stays legible and quiet.
- Use a dark high-contrast pill CTA with an inset arrow chip against a pastel field — it pops without a saturated button color and feels premium.
- Slip a positioning pivot into the subhead ('for technical teams and agents') so a friendly consumer-feeling brand quietly claims a serious dev/AI audience.

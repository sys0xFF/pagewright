# Campsite — https://campsite.design

![campsite.design](../thumbs/campsite.design.jpg)

- **niche:** productivity
- **mood:** clean-light
- **style:** minimal, clean, light
- **palette:** bg `#FFFFFF` · ink `#1A1A1A` · accent `#F1421B` — logo mark, the second announcement pill (full fill), inline link arrows/chips, and the warm ember gradient bleeding behind the product screenshot
- **type:** display *Söhne / GT America-style grotesque (geometric sans, tight tracking)* · body *Same grotesque sans, lighter weight* — Crisp, confident, neutral-modern; near-zero letterspacing, large optical size, no flourish
- **sections:** hero › feature-posts-positioning › feature-transparency › feature-references › feature-notifications › feature-messaging › feature-integrations › footer
- **signature:** Twin stacked pill announcement banners floating dead-center above the headline — one black "NEW Campsite is winding down," one solid-orange "Campsite's founders have joined Notion" — each with a circular arrow chip, turning the product's own sunset into the first thing you see.
- **imagery:** Single oversized product-screenshot of the app's Home feed (sidebar + post stream + presence panel), rendered crisp and full-fidelity, anchored to the bottom of the hero and bleeding off the lower edge so it reads as a real window you're peering into. A faint warm orange ember/spark gradient leaks up from the bottom-left corner behind the UI, tying back to the campfire logo.
- **copy:** Aspirational two-line antithesis headline — "Teamwork, meet deep work" — framing the product as a reconciliation of two opposing modes; calm, declarative, manifesto-lite voice with a problem-named subhead ("replace noisy chats with focused, organized posts").

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Stack two announcement pills above the H1 (one neutral, one accent-filled) to layer a status update over a value prop without a banner bar stealing the hero.
- Center-align the entire hero — pills, headline, subhead — then break the symmetry by docking one giant product screenshot flush to the bottom edge so it bleeds out of frame.
- Leak a single warm gradient (sampled from the logo color) up from one corner behind an otherwise pure-white UI shot — just enough warmth to avoid clinical-flat without adding any decoration.
- Write the headline as a two-word antithesis ('X, meet Y') that names the tension your product resolves, and let the subhead do the literal 'replace A with B' explanation.

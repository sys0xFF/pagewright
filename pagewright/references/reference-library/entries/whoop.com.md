**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Set the headline huge and wrap it to several deliberately tight lines so the copy itself becomes the hero's compositional mass over a quiet photo.
- Keep the brand accent off the in-hero CTA — reserve the saturated color for the announcement bar and nav pill so it signals "system/brand" not "ad."
- Convert a dry commercial detail (HSA/FSA eligibility) into a small hero badge to defuse price objection right above the fold.
- Use a desaturated, shallow-focus lifestyle photo with the product implied rather than shown — sells the feeling/outcome instead of the hardware.

**Takeaways (steal as ideas, don't copy):**
- Two-tone headline: set the second clause in a muted tint of the same color so one keyword (here 'burden') recedes — adds rhythm without a second font or weight.
- Dramatize the mechanism literally: instead of a generic 3D shape, render the actual artifact your product protects (a card) emerging from a beam of light cutting through encrypted-character noise.
- Keep CTAs white-on-dark and reserve the saturated accent purely for atmosphere (gradient wash + light beam), so buttons read as the brightest tap target on a dark page.
- Anchor the dark hero with a single bottom-up violet gradient bloom behind the copy — it lifts text legibility and gives an otherwise flat near-black page a focal glow.

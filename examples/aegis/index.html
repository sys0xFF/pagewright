[ 01 ] The reality of the 3am page

By the time a human reads the alert, the attacker has already moved laterally.

The average dwell time before detection is still measured in days. Legacy EDR floods your queue with thousands of low-context alerts, and your best analysts burn out triaging noise instead of stopping intrusions. The gap between seeing and stopping is where breaches happen.

Legacy EDR
  • Thousands of alerts a day, no path context — triage is guesswork.
  • Response waits on a human to log in, investigate, and click isolate.
  • Detections live in a console nobody opens until the breach is news.
The Aegis way
  • Every detection arrives as a full attack story — entry, path, blast radius.
  • The host is isolated in 0.8s by policy — the page comes after containment.
  • Your analysts review a clean, ranked queue — signal, not noise.

[ 06 ] Before you ask

The questions a CISO actually asks.

Will autonomous isolation take down a production server by mistake?

No qualifying action runs until you've armed it. You start in observe-only mode and watch exactly what Aegis would have done. Critical assets can be tagged "page-only," so they alert a human instead of self-isolating. Every action is reversible from the console in one click.

How heavy is the sensor on endpoint performance?

The agent holds under 1% sustained CPU and ~80MB of memory. Telemetry is filtered on-device, so you ship signal — not raw firehose — to the cloud. Users won't notice it's there; your finance team will notice the egress bill didn't explode.

Does it replace our SIEM, or sit alongside it?

Alongside. Aegis is the endpoint sensor and response layer; it streams enriched, correlated detections into Splunk, Sentinel, Elastic, or your data lake via native connectors. Many teams cut SIEM ingest cost because we forward incidents, not noise.

What compliance frameworks do you cover?

SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and FedRAMP Moderate (in process). Every autonomous action writes an immutable audit record, and incident reports export to the evidence format your assessors expect. Data residency available in US, EU, and APAC regions.

How long does migration from our current EDR take?

Aegis runs in parallel with your incumbent during cutover, so you're never unprotected. Most teams are fully migrated in two to three weeks; the median time to first contained threat is the same afternoon you deploy.

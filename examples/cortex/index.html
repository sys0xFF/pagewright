Answers

Before you ask

Still curious? Talk to a human →

How fast can we be live?＋

Most teams send their first events in under five minutes with autocapture, and have a working funnel before standup. No data model to design up front.

Do I need to know SQL?＋

No. Funnels, retention, and breakdowns are point-and-click, and Ask Cortex turns plain-English questions into real charts. If you do want SQL, there’s a full query editor and warehouse sync.

Is it secure and compliant?＋

Cortex is SOC 2 Type II and GDPR-ready, with SSO/SAML, granular roles, audit logs, and EU/US data residency on Enterprise. You control PII redaction at ingest.

How is pricing calculated?＋

By events ingested, not seats — so you can invite the whole company without it getting expensive. The first 1M events every month are free, and you set a hard cap so there are no surprise bills.

Can we migrate off Amplitude or Mixpanel?＋

Yes. Keep your existing taxonomy via Segment, backfill history from your warehouse, and run both side-by-side during the switch. Our team helps map your events for free.

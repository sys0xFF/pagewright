How it is kept

The hour, in four steps.

No ten-step regimen. Four unhurried movements, most evenings — the practice is the point.

  1. 01

    Cleanse, then wait

    Leave the skin barely damp. The serum draws that water in rather than sealing it out.

  2. 02

    Warm three drops

    Between the palms, until the oil loosens and the botanicals open. Breathe once.

  3. 03

    Press, don't rub

    Cheeks, jaw, throat — palms flat. A slower hand leaves the skin calmer.

  4. 04

    Leave it be

    No follow-up cream needed. Let the room go quiet. The hour is yours now.

Stop losing cash to idle balances

Most of a startup's bank balance earns nothing. Atlas puts that money to work the day it lands, without trading liquidity for yield.

Backed by US Treasuries3

Your treasury balance is invested in a portfolio of short-dated government securities held in your company's name, not lent out on a bank's balance sheet.

Up to $5M in coverage4

Operating deposits are swept across a network of program banks so eligible balances carry FDIC coverage well past the standard limit.

Withdraw any business day

No lockups and no penalty. Pull treasury cash back to operating same-day, so a payroll run or a surprise bill never waits on a settlement window.
